Usage Guide

WeatherNaut is designed to make accessing weather information quick and easy. Here's how to make the most of our service:

  1. Search by City: Use the search bar to look up the weather for a specific city. Enter the city name and click "Search".
  2. Select from Popular Cities: You can also choose from popular cities like Ayodhya, Rudraprayag, Udupi, and Bangalore by clicking on them in the dropdown menu.
  3. Explore Detailed Information: Once you've selected a city, WeatherNaut will provide you with detailed weather information, including temperature, humidity, cloud cover, feels like temperature, and more.
  4. Compare Multiple Cities: The "Other Cities" section allows you to compare weather data for different cities, making it easy to plan trips or stay informed about various locations.

WeatherNaut aims to be a user-friendly tool for accessing accurate and reliable weather data. Whether you're a casual user or someone who relies on weather information for daily planning, we're here to help.
